Frequently Asked Questions

What can I expect in a coaching session?

In our coaching sessions, you can expect a safe, confidential space where we'll explore your challenges, set meaningful goals, and develop practical strategies to achieve them. Each session is tailored to your unique needs and focuses on actionable steps for growth and transformation.

How long does the coaching process take?

The duration of coaching varies based on individual needs and goals. Some clients achieve their desired results in just a few sessions, while others benefit from longer-term support. We'll regularly assess your progress and adjust our approach as needed to ensure you're moving forward effectively.

Is coaching the same as therapy?

While both coaching and therapy can be valuable, they serve different purposes. Coaching primarily focuses on present challenges and future goals, helping you develop strategies for personal and professional growth. Therapy often addresses past traumas and mental health conditions. As both a psychologist and coach, I can help determine which approach would best serve your needs.

How do I know if I need a life coach?

You might benefit from life coaching if you're feeling stuck, seeking clarity about your goals, navigating a life transition, or wanting to enhance your personal or professional development. If you have a vision for your life but are struggling to make it a reality, coaching can provide the structure, accountability, and support you need to move forward.

What's your coaching style?

My coaching approach is holistic, empathetic, and solution-focused. I combine psychological insights with practical strategies to help you overcome obstacles and achieve meaningful growth. I believe in creating a supportive yet challenging environment where you can explore your potential, build resilience, and develop the skills needed to thrive in all areas of life.
